It was released on March 24, 2015 for PC/Mac, PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 3, on March 25th for Xbox One and Xbox 360, and on March 26th for iOS and Android. </p> <p><br></p> <h2 id="Plot" section="1">Plot</h2> <p><i><a href="/wiki/Asher_Forrester" title="Asher Forrester">Asher</a>, the exile, heads to <a href="/wiki/Meereen" title="Meereen">Meereen</a> in search of an army to take on the <a href="/wiki/House_Whitehill" title="House Whitehill">Whitehills</a>. Meanwhile, far across land and sea in <a href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mira_Forrester" title="Mira Forrester">Mira</a> must deal with the lethal politics of <a href="/wiki/King%27s_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>. Her family's safety is paramount and she will do anything to protect them, but nothing is given freely, and her choice of allies may soon come to haunt her. To the north, in <a href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a>, the Whitehill occupation continues. <a href="/wiki/Gryff_Whitehill" title="Gryff Whitehill">Gryff Whitehill</a>, fourth-born son, is out to prove himself, and brutality and violence grows daily, pushing the Forresters to make far-reaching decisions. Finally, at <a href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gared_Tuttle" title="Gared Tuttle">Gared</a> learns that he must head north if he is to help his house survive. But fate is cruel, forcing choices that will change his path forever.</i> </p> <h2 id="Summary" section="2">Summary</h2> <h3 id="Across_the_Narrow_Sea" section="3">Across the <a href="/wiki/Narrow_Sea" title="Narrow Sea">Narrow Sea</a> </h3> <p>Asher, Beshka, and Malcolm are journeying to <a href="/wiki/Meereen" title="Meereen">Meereen</a>, whilst the <a href="/wiki/Lost_Legion" title="Lost Legion">Lost Legion</a> hunt them. The group is traveling in a ravine, when their advance is blocked by some rubble. Asher finds a cave, however, and the group comes in, with the Lost Legion right on their tails. Unknown to Asher, the cave they entered is <a href="/wiki/Drogon" title="Drogon">Drogon's</a> lair. Drogon ambushes Asher, Beshka, and Malcolm, as well as the Lost Legion who are entering his lair. In the ensuing chaos, Malcolm is surrounded by Legion men, while Beskha facing Drogon. The Asher makes a choice to either help Beskha or Malcolm. The one who isn't saved is burned, and is helped by Asher out of the cave. Drogon flies out of the cave, and into the sky. </p> <h3 id="At_The_Wall" section="4">At <a href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">The Wall</a> </h3> <p><i>TBA</i> </p> <h3 id="In_King.27s_Landing"
